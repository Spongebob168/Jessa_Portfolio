Reflection: ICT Policies and Online Safety

From this activity, I learned that ICT policies help guide the proper and responsible use of technology in education. They also protect users from risks such as cyberbullying, privacy violations, and misuse of information online. I realized that online safety is very important for both teachers and students when using digital tools and the internet.

Following ICT policies helps maintain a safe, respectful, and secure learning environment. It is important for teachers and students to follow these rules to avoid problems and ensure responsible digital behavior.

As a future teacher, I can apply these policies by reminding students to practice proper netiquette, respect privacy, and use technology responsibly during learning activities. In this way, technology can be used safely and effectively to support teaching and learning.
